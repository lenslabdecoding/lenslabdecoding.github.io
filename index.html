Decoding viewer demo

This demo illustrates some of the functionality of the Decoder viewer. The viewer can be easily dropped into any webpage; in this example, we add a bunch of views and components, and then load three layers: (i) a standard MNI152 anatomical template; (ii) an anatomically defined region from the Harvard-Oxford atlas; and (iii) and a ranked list of stimulus based on decoding strength. Layers can be turned on or off and dynamically resorted by dragging and dropping.
